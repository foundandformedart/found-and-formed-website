our story

courtney
& michael

found & formed was born out of a shared leap of faith. courtney has always been an artist at heart — while building a career as a commercial interior designer, she quietly carried the desire to make a living from what she truly loved: art. over time, burnout made it clear — something needed to change.

at the same time, michael was living out his dream as a professional soccer player, faithfully supporting courtney as she pursued the calling God placed on her life — to create freely and build something that invites others to do the same.

found & formed is the result of that step of faith. more than a brand — it's a space. a place to rest, create, and reconnect, where perfection isn't required and joy is the goal.

"when you give yourself permission to create — not for performance, but for joy — you begin to remember who you are."

courtney

michael
